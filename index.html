About
Plasma is a cutting-edge blockchain designed specifically for stablecoins, offering top-tier performance, scalability, and security. Unlike older blockchains created before stablecoins became popular, Plasma is tailored to meet their unique needs. With stablecoins now boasting over $225 billion in circulation and handling trillions in monthly transactions, they’re a cornerstone of the crypto world, and Plasma is built to power this vital use case.
Roadmap
Phase 1 – Mainnet Beta: Launch Plasma with a high-speed PlasmaBFT consensus and EVM-compatible layer for smart contracts, setting a solid base for stablecoin issuers, liquidity providers, and fintech partners. Phase 2 – Bitcoin Bridge: Add a trustless Bitcoin Bridge to connect Plasma to Bitcoin, enabling smooth liquidity and interoperability. Phase 3 – Stablecoin Features: Roll out custom gas tokens, free USDT transfers, private transactions, while boosting speed and usability. Phase 4 – Developer Tools: Release APIs, SDKs, and tools to help developers and financial institutions integrate with Plasma’s stablecoin-focused blockchain.
